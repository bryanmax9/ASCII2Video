// PROCESS

  
How It Works

  
Three steps from raw video to glowing ASCII art. All processing happens locally in your browser.


  

    

      
01

      
Drop Your Video

      
Upload any .mp4, .mov, .webm, .avi, or .mkv file. Works entirely in the browser ‚ÄĒ your file never leaves your device.

    

    

      
02

      
Frame Sampling

      
Each frame is drawn to a canvas, pixel data extracted, and luminance calculated using the ITU-R BT.601 formula: 0.299R + 0.587G + 0.114B.

    

    

      
03

      
Character Mapping

      
Luminance maps to a 70-character density ramp from space to @. Lower threshold reveals more detail; invert flips dark/light.

    

    

      
04

      
Animate & Export

      
All frames play back at your chosen FPS. Change colors, adjust columns, and watch your video rendered in pure text.
